    A Grapefruit Slice

    September 10, 2007 by Laurian

    My 20% of Grapefruit srl, are for sale.

    Grapefruit (www​.grapefruit​.ro) is a Romanian branding consultancy, with offices in Iasi and Bucharest. The Grapefruit portfolio holds more than 100 clients in 13 countries on 4 continents, including: Abbott Laboratories, Antibiotice, Dacia, Vodafone, HP Romania, Gândul, Eli Lilly, OTP Bank, British Council, International Corporate and Brand Identity Centre (Great Britain), and DDB San Francisco (USA). Grapefruit was the most widely nominated and awarded agency at this year’s edition of Identity: Best of the Best, the international competition for logo and corporate identity design organized by Moscow’s Identity magazine.
