    Ten Years

    November 13, 2009 by Laurian

    Today Grapefruit turned 10.

    Ten years ago, me, Marius Ursache and Stefan Călin Liuțe joined forces and created Grapefruit (back then called ‘Grapefruit Design’). We were the first in Romania to tell and do many things about corporate and brand identity, naming, brand manuals and web sites.

    We were terrible stubborn in everything, particularly I was terrible stubborn about my technological choices — we were also among the first creative agencies in Romania to work with Java Servlets and XSLT and avoided as much as possible CGI, PHP and ASP which were popular among the other web agencies. Having already several years of Java experience and especially knowing XSLT were instrumental in working with Connex (now Vodafone Romania), with Hewlett-​Packard Romania for the first Romanian mobile portal, and for many other projects.

    Looking back, there were countless of projects, each of them with their interesting story, with the endless arguments with clients’ IT departments and with fellow colleagues on why Java, why XSLT, why DocBook, etc. — I knew that it wasn’t technology for technology’s sake, but I realise now what it was: the quest for a comfortable level of abstraction…
