    Ubiquity and “The Semantic Web”

    September 16, 2009 by Laurian

    Aza Raskin on conversational computing and “The Semantic Web” (loving the quotes)

    OK, how can we have Ubiquity publish what people map (with their permission, of course), what commands they use on what piece of content?

    Think URL, in page location (Annotea–like XPointer?), command applied (therefore identifying the type of the selected snippet) … can we have Ubiquity “plugins” that listen to what happens in Ubiquity?

    I was playing this summer with Ubiquity and rdfQuery, I’ve got Annotea-​like locations working with RDFa integration, all I need is how to hook it deeper into Ubiquity …
